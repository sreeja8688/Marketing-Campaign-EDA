# 📊 Marketing Campaign EDA

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Marketing Campaign dataset to evaluate the performance of different marketing channels. The primary objective is to identify which channels deliver the highest Return on Investment (ROI) and provide data-driven recommendations for marketing budget allocation.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
- Analyze campaign performance using EDA.
- Visualize key marketing metrics.
- Study the marketing funnel (Impressions → Clicks → Leads → Conversions).
- Compare marketing channels based on Revenue, ROI, Acquisition Cost, and Conversions.
- Recommend the best marketing channels for future budget allocation.

---

## 📂 Dataset Features

The dataset includes the following information:

- Campaign Type
- Channel Used
- Impressions
- Clicks
- Leads
- Conversions
- Revenue
- Acquisition Cost
- ROI
- Customer Segment
- Date

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning

The following preprocessing steps were performed:

- Imported the dataset using Pandas.
- Checked dataset structure and data types.
- Identified and handled missing values.
- Removed duplicate records.
- Converted the Date column into datetime format.
- Cleaned and processed the marketing channel data for analysis.

---

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis

The following visualizations were created:

- Marketing Channel Distribution
- Average Revenue by Marketing Channel
- Average ROI by Marketing Channel
- Average Acquisition Cost by Marketing Channel
- Average Conversions by Marketing Channel
- Correlation Heatmap
- Marketing Funnel Analysis

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Compared the performance of different marketing channels.
- Evaluated campaign profitability using ROI.
- Identified channels generating higher revenue and conversions.
- Analyzed customer drop-off through the marketing funnel.
- Explored relationships between important numerical variables.

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in marketing channels with higher ROI.
- Continue investing in channels generating high revenue and conversions.
- Optimize campaigns with high acquisition costs but lower ROI.
- Improve customer engagement to reduce drop-off in the marketing funnel.
- Monitor campaign performance regularly to support data-driven decisions.
